Find Your Scheme

Q. How do I find out which government schemes I am eligible for?

Use our "Eligibility Checker" tool. Enter your personal details and requirements, and it will display the schemes you qualify for.

Q. What information do I need to apply for a scheme?

Each scheme has different requirements. Generally, you’ll need personal identification, proof of income, and relevant documents based on the scheme. Check the specific requirements listed on the scheme’s page.

Q. How can I track the status of my application?

After submitting your application, go to the "Application Status" section. Enter your application number to see the current status and any updates.

Q. Are there any deadlines for applying to these schemes?

Yes, each scheme has its own deadlines. You can find the deadline information in the details section of each scheme.

Q. What should I do if I encounter problems while applying online?

If you experience issues, visit our "Support" section or contact our helpline for assistance.
