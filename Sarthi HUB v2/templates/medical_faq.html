Find Your medical Path

Q. How can I book an appointment with a doctor online?

Visit our "Appointments" section, choose a doctor, and select a suitable date and time for your consultation.

Q. What services are available through remote consultations?

Our remote consultations cover general health inquiries, specialist consultations, follow-up appointments, and mental health support

Q. How do I access my medical records online?

Log in to your account and navigate to the "My Records" section to view and manage your medical records securely.

Q.What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

For emergencies, please call the emergency services number immediately. Our platform is for non-urgent consultations and advice only.

Q.Can I get prescriptions through online consultations?

Yes, our doctors can prescribe medications during your consultation if deemed necessary.
